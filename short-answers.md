1. Difference between Google Knowledge Graph and Knowledge Panel

The Google Knowledge Graph is a database of entities (people, companies, places) and their relationships.

The Knowledge Panel is the visual box shown in search results that displays information from the Knowledge Graph.


 2. How Google Determines Entity Identity

Google determines entity identity using a combination of signals such as structured data (schema markup), consistent brand information across platforms, and authoritative mentions.

It also relies on sameAs links that connect social profiles, as well as user behavior like branded searches.

These signals help Google confirm that an entity is real, trustworthy, and clearly defined.


 3. When to Create Custom Post Types Instead of Pages

Custom Post Types should be used when the content is repeatable and needs a structured format, such as portfolios, listings, or testimonials.

They are useful when you need custom fields, filtering, or separate archives.

Pages are better suited for static content like About or Contact pages.


4. Recommended Plugins for Speed Optimization and Why

LiteSpeed Cache is recommended for its powerful caching, image optimization, and overall performance improvements.

WP Rocket is also a strong option due to its ease of use and effective speed optimization features.

Autoptimize helps in minimizing CSS, JavaScript, and HTML files to improve load time.

These plugins enhance website speed, which improves user experience and search engine rankings.
